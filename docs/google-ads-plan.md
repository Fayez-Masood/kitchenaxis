## 1. Account Structure

**Principles:** separate campaigns by intent (emergency vs planned) and by language (EN vs AR); themed ad groups by service; geo at campaign level.

| Campaign | Language | Intent | Ad groups |

| **1. Emergency — EN** | English | Urgent | `AG-Emergency-Repair`, `AG-Cold-Room-Down` |

| **3. Repair & Maintenance — EN** | English | Planned | `AG-Cooking-Repair`, `AG-Refrigeration`, `AG-Coffee-Beverage`, `AG-General-Maintenance` |
| **4. Repair & Maintenance — AR** | Arabic | Planned | (same 4, Arabic) |

| **6. AMC & Installation — AR** | Arabic | Contracts/fit-out | `AG-AMC`, `AG-Installation` |

Naming: `KA | {Intent} | {Lang} | {Service}`. Apply labels (`lang:*`, `intent:*`, `service:*`).
Landing pages: Emergency → home `#contact` (call/WhatsApp above fold); service AGs → matching anchor; AMC → `#amc`; AR ads → AR locale URL.

### Phase 2 — after ~3-4 weeks of conversion data
- **Call-only campaign (EN+AR)** for emergency mobile terms, 24/7.
- **Performance Max** once 30+ conversions (keep separate; add brand + top exact keywords as PMax account-level negatives).
- **Retargeting** (Demand Gen/Display), AMC/warranty message.
- **Brand campaign** (low budget, exact/phrase) once traffic exists.

## 6. Budget & Bidding

CPCs roughly **SAR 4-14** on service terms, **SAR 10-20+** on emergency/near-me.

| Campaign group | Daily (SAR) | Monthly (SAR) |
|---|---|---|
| Emergency (EN+AR) | 80-120 | ~2,400-3,600 |
| Repair & Maintenance (EN+AR) | 100-150 | ~3,000-4,500 |
| AMC & Installation (EN+AR) | 40-70 | ~1,200-2,100 |
| **Total launch** | **~220-340/day** | **~6,600-10,200/mo** |

Start near the low-mid end (~SAR 220-260/day ≈ SAR 7,000/mo).
**Bidding — staged:** (1) Weeks 1-2 Maximize Clicks + CPC cap (SAR 12-16) or Manual CPC to prove tracking. (2) Weeks 2-4 (~15 conv) → Maximize Conversions. (3) Week 4+ (~30 conv/30 days) → Target CPA (~SAR 60-150/lead; emergency higher). Emergency moves to tCPA fastest; AMC stays on Max Conversions longer.
**Scaling:** raise budget ~20% every 3-4 days on profitable, budget-limited campaigns; if impr. share lost to budget >10-15% on a winner, add spend; hold on rising CPA.

## 7. Conversion Tracking Plan

The site already loads GA4 + Ads gtag and fires **`generate_lead`** on form submit, with a ready path to fire the Ads **`conversion`** event (`send_to = AW-ID/label`).

### Create in Google Ads → Goals → Conversions

| # | Action | Type | Category | Primary? | Value |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Website Lead — Form | Website (gtag) | Submit lead form | **Primary** | SAR 150 |
| 2 | Phone Call — From Ads | Calls from ads | Phone call lead | **Primary** | SAR 150 |
| 3 | Website Call — Number Click | Website | Phone call lead | **Primary** | SAR 150 |
| 4 | WhatsApp Click | Website | Contact | Secondary | — |
| 5 | Lead-Form Asset Submit | (auto) | Submit lead form | **Primary** | SAR 150 |

Only Primary actions drive Smart Bidding. Counting = "One" for leads.

**Map to existing code:** When you create action #1, Google gives a Conversion ID + label. Put in env:
```
NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_ADS_ID=AW-XXXXXXXXX
NEXT_PUBLIC_ADS_LEAD_LABEL=abcDEF123
```
The form success handler already fires both `generate_lead` (GA4) and the Ads `conversion` (`send_to = ${ID}/${label}`) — **no further site code needed** once env vars are set. Phone-call-from-ads (#2) is configured on the call asset (no code). Website call-click (#3) and WhatsApp (#4) can be wired to `tel:`/`wa.me` click handlers with their own labels (`NEXT_PUBLIC_ADS_CALL_LABEL`, `NEXT_PUBLIC_ADS_WHATSAPP_LABEL`) or via GTM.

**Verify** with Tag Assistant on both EN + AR URLs before spending. Conversion window 30 days; attribution = data-driven.

**Language:** EN campaigns = English, AR campaigns = Arabic; keep ads/keywords single-language per campaign; watch the search-terms report and cross-negative wrong-language queries.
**Device:** start neutral; after 2 weeks consider +15-30% mobile on Emergency; prioritize call-only for mobile emergency.
