# KitchenAxis SEO Strategy

Launch-ready organic search strategy for **kitchenaxis.co** — bilingual (EN/AR-RTL) commercial kitchen equipment service across Saudi Arabia. Current build is a single long landing page per language with a strong technical baseline (sitemap, robots, hreflang, LocalBusiness JSON-LD, canonicals, GA4/Ads). The core strategic gap is that **one page cannot rank for both emergency, city-modified, and equipment-specific intents simultaneously** — this plan fixes that with a lightweight page/cluster architecture layered on the existing site.

**Rule (cannibalization guard):** The home page owns the head term "commercial kitchen equipment repair/maintenance (KSA)". City pages own "**{service} {city}**" and must NOT repeat the bare head term in their title/H1. Service pages own "**{equipment} repair**" and must NOT use a city in the H1. This keeps one owner per query and prevents the home page and city pages from splitting clicks.

## 3. Local SEO

### 3.1 Google Business Profile (GBP) — setup checklist

- [ ] **Primary category:** `Commercial refrigeration` OR `Appliance repair service` (test both; "Appliance repair service" surfaces more service-area behavior in KSA).
- [ ] **Secondary categories:** `Refrigerator repair service`, `Air conditioning repair service` (chillers), `Repair service`, `Commercial refrigeration`.
- [ ] **Business type:** Service-area business (SAB) — hide the street address if you don't take walk-ins; list service areas = Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Makkah, Madinah.
- [ ] **Name (NAP):** Exactly `KitchenAxis` — do not keyword-stuff ("KitchenAxis - Commercial Kitchen Repair") as that risks suspension.
- [ ] **Phone:** `+966 53 548 9318` — identical to site + footer + schema, everywhere.
- [ ] **Website:** `https://kitchenaxis.co`.
- [ ] **Hours:** Sat–Thu 8am–8pm; add **"Open 24 hours"** for the emergency line via a separate attribute/description note.
- [ ] **Services list:** Add each service as a GBP service item with description (cooking equipment repair, refrigeration & cold room repair, coffee/beverage repair, installation & commissioning, AMC, 24/7 emergency).
- [ ] **Attributes:** "Online estimates", "Onsite services", identifies-as (if applicable).
- [ ] **Products:** Add the 3 AMC plans (Essential/Professional/Enterprise) as GBP products.
- [ ] **Photos:** Upload 20+ **original** on-site job photos (you already shoot real work — big advantage). Geo-tag before upload; name files descriptively (`cold-room-repair-riyadh.jpg`). Add technician + van/branding shots for trust.
- [ ] **Bilingual:** Set GBP language to Arabic (primary audience) with an English description too.

### 3.2 NAP consistency

Publish identical NAP on: footer, contact section, GBP, and top 5–10 KSA directories (Google Maps, Bing Places, Daleeli, Yellow Pages KSA, Foursquare, Apple Business Connect). Add `CR No.` and `VAT No.` to the footer once available — these are strong KSA trust/E-E-A-T signals.

### 3.3 Reviews strategy

- Target **10 reviews in first 60 days**, then ~4/month. Prioritize Arabic-language reviews from named businesses ("مطعم/فندق X").
- Post-job automation: CS+ widget or a WhatsApp follow-up sends a GBP review short-link after the service report is delivered.
- Respond to **every** review in the reviewer's language within 48h; naturally reference city + service ("سعدنا بخدمة غرفة التبريد في الرياض").
- Never gate/incentivize reviews (violates Google policy).

### 3.4 Per-city landing pages — **YES, build them**

A single SAB GBP cannot rank organically for "…Riyadh / …Jeddah" across 5 cities. Dedicated indexable city pages are the highest-ROI structural move.

**Recommended structure** (Next.js route per city, both locales):

```
/en/riyadh   ·  /ar/riyadh   (+ jeddah, dammam, makkah, madinah)
```

Each city page (min 500–700 words unique copy — do **not** template-spin):
- H1: `Commercial Kitchen Equipment Repair in {City}` / `صيانة معدات المطابخ التجارية بـ{المدينة}`
- Local proof: neighborhoods/districts served, a city-specific testimonial, real local job photos.
- Services offered in that city + response-time promise ("technician on-site in {City} within 2 hours").
- City-specific FAQPage schema + LocalBusiness with `areaServed = {City}`.
- Internal links: up ↔ home, across ↔ relevant service pages.
- CTA: call/WhatsApp/form (form pre-fills city).

Avoid doorway-page penalties: each page must have genuinely unique local content, not find-and-replace.

## 4. Structured Data

You have LocalBusiness — layer these on top (JSON-LD, injected per page/locale):

1. **Service** (one per service page): `serviceType`, `provider` (→ your LocalBusiness `@id`), `areaServed` (the 5 cities), `hasOfferCatalog` listing sub-services. Link via `@id` to avoid duplicate entities.
2. **FAQPage** (home, AMC, each service + city page): mark up 4–6 real Q&As. This is your fastest route to People-Also-Ask and AI-overview citations. Use the informational keywords from §1.5 as questions. Provide the AR version on AR pages.
3. **BreadcrumbList** (all sub-pages once the architecture exists): Home → Services → {Service}, and Home → {City}.
4. **AggregateRating / Review** — add **only after** you have real, verifiable GBP/site reviews with names. Do not fabricate.
5. **Organization** (home): `name`, `logo`, `url`, `sameAs` (Instagram/LinkedIn/X once populated in `site.ts`), `contactPoint` with `telephone`, `availableLanguage: [ar, en]`, `areaServed: SA`.
6. **Offer / AggregateOffer** on AMC plans: 3 `Offer` nodes (Essential/Professional/Enterprise) with `priceCurrency: SAR`.
7. **WebSite + potentialAction (SearchAction)** — optional; low priority.

Validate every type in the Rich Results Test and Schema Markup Validator per locale. Ensure Arabic schema uses Arabic values and matching `inLanguage: ar`.

## 5. Content Gaps / Topic Clusters

The site is all bottom-funnel. Add a lightweight cluster. All bilingual.

**Cluster 1 — Service hubs (money pages):** `/services/cooking-equipment`, `/services/refrigeration-cold-rooms`, `/services/coffee-beverage`, `/services/installation-commissioning`, `/maintenance-contracts` (AMC), `/emergency` (24/7 landing page for all E-intent).

**Cluster 2 — City pages** (§3.4): 5 cities × 2 locales = 10 pages.

**Cluster 3 — Guides / troubleshooting:** fryer not heating; cold room not cooling; oven temperature inaccurate; how often to service; preventive-maintenance checklist (downloadable = linkable asset); what an AMC covers; ice machine not producing; dishwasher not draining.

**Internal linking:** every guide → parent service hub + `/emergency` CTA; service hubs → relevant city pages; city pages → service hubs; home → all 6 hubs. Add all new URLs (EN+AR) to `sitemap.xml` with hreflang pairs.
