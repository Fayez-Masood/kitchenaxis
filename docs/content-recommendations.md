*Reviewed against the live copy in `lib/dictionaries/en.ts`, `ar.ts`, and facts in `lib/site.ts` (2-hour response, 10+ years, 200+ kitchens, 5 cities, +966 53 548 9318).*

> Two defects flagged here are **already fixed** in code: the Arabic dual-form grammar ("ساعتين" not "2 ساعات") now uses a shared `RH` helper, and the EN "costs you covers" line was corrected. The rest below are recommendations to apply when you're ready.

## 1. Content Audit — Honest Critique

### Strong
- Clear B2B positioning ("Commercial kitchen specialists · Saudi Arabia", "one accountable partner").
- Trust scaffolding present (certified techs, 2-hour response, warranty, genuine parts, service reports).
- The Arabic is genuinely good — not machine-literal in most places.

### Weaknesses / gaps
1. **Hero headline underperforms.** "Commercial kitchens don't get downtime." is clever but ambiguous and keyword-free (no "repair/maintenance/Riyadh").
2. **No numbers where they convert** — great stats (10+ yrs, 200+ kitchens) never appear in the hero/subhead.
3. **~~EN typo "costs you covers"~~** — fixed.
4. **Flat verbs** ("serviced to spec", "run") — little emotional stake (spoiled stock, failed inspection, lost covers).
5. **No FAQ** — a big SEO/AEO + Ads Quality Score miss.
6. **Thin trust layer** — no brands serviced, certifications, or explicit guarantees; testimonials are placeholder.
7. **AMC has no price anchoring** — three tiers, no "from SAR X".
8. **SEO gaps** — no city/equipment keywords in headings; EN meta title too long.
9. **CTA sameness** — emergency vs planned visitors want different buttons.

## 7. Localization Notes

1. **~~Response-time dual-form grammar~~** — fixed (shared `RH` = "ساعتين").
2. **Brand name consistency** — decide `كيتشن أكسِس` vs Latin `KitchenAxis` in Arabic; use one everywhere (Latin is clean/modern and matches the logo).
3. **Stat label** `مطبخ نخدمه` → `مطبخ خدمناه` reads better as a completed count.
4. **`المطابخ السحابية`** is the accepted KSA term for cloud/ghost kitchens — safe to introduce.
5. Keep register in MSA (`فصحى`), avoid dialect; imperatives (`احجز`, `اطلب`, `اتصل`) are the right punchy tone.
6. **Numbers/RTL** — ensure the phone number renders LTR inside RTL (bidi-isolation) so it doesn't reverse.
